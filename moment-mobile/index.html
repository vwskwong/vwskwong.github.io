Moment

Use your past moments
to better the next

React Native mobile application that aims to motivate users on their pursuits of passion through periodic notifications of user inputs of their own goals, personal struggles and successes, and inspirational quotes.

Dashboard
Users receive new motivational quotes and lifestyle tips every day! A carousel displays their moments and they can navigate to routes for managing their moments and settings.
Moments
To remind them of their sparks of passion, users can input their goals, inspirational quotes, and their past personal struggles and successes. These entries are stored locally in JSON format and are sent back to the user in periodic notifications to motivate them on their pursuit of passion.
Your Moments
Users are able to manage their moments by pressing on the desired entry. A collapsible component will be displayed with the option of editing or deleting the entry.
Settings
Users can specify when to start, how often, and when to stop receiving periodic notifications of their moments. Some features including sending feedback and account settings are currently being implemented with AWS.
Behind Moment
Remember when that twelve year old thanked you for being a great role model? Remember when you were so sure of your abilities, yet you lost the competition? The overwhelming sense of accomplishment that made you feel as if nothing could stop you. How disappointed you were in yourself and the swearing of redemption. Don't forget these sparks of passion and emotion.
